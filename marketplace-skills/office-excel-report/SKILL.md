# Office Excel Report

## Workflow
1. Identify key dimensions (time, team, product, region) and KPI columns.
2. Compute trend, period-over-period variance, and top/bottom contributors.
3. Summarize insights in business language for non-technical stakeholders.
4. Add anomaly checks and next validation actions.

## Output Structure
- KPI Snapshot
- What improved
- What declined
- Risks/Anomalies
- Recommended actions
