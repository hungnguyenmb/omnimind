# Office Task Planner

## Workflow
1. Break goals into milestones, then into deliverable tasks.
2. Prioritize using urgency and impact.
3. Build a realistic week plan with owner and due date per task.
4. Provide daily standup format: yesterday, today, blockers.

## Output Structure
- Weekly priorities (top 3)
- Task board by day
- Risks and mitigations
- Review checklist for Friday
