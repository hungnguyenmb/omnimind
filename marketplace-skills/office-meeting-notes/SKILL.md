# Office Meeting Notes

## Workflow
1. Normalize notes by topic, speaker, and timeline.
2. Produce four sections: Summary, Decisions, Risks, Action Items.
3. For each action item, capture owner, due date, and dependency when available.
4. End with a short recap email draft for attendees.

## Output Template
- Summary: 3-6 lines.
- Decisions: numbered list.
- Risks/Blockers: concise bullets.
- Action Items: table-like bullets `Owner | Task | Due`.
